User Flow

Overview

The Pravas app is designed with a user-centric approach, ensuring a seamless and intuitive experience from onboarding to post-trip feedback. This document outlines the complete user journey through the application, highlighting key interaction points and features.

1. User Registration & Onboarding

Step 1: Download & Install

  • User downloads the app from Google Play Store or Apple App Store
  • App installation with minimal required permissions

Step 2: User Registration

  • Option to register using email, phone number, or social media accounts
  • Basic profile creation with name and contact information
  • Optional: Creation of blockchain wallet for secure transactions

Step 3: Preference Setting

  • Selection of preferred language (English, Hindi, or local languages)
  • Interest selection (nature, culture, adventure, food, etc.)
  • Travel style preferences (budget, luxury, eco-friendly, etc.)
  • Special requirements (accessibility needs, dietary restrictions)

Step 4: App Tour

  • Interactive walkthrough of key features
  • Introduction to AI assistant and chatbot
  • Brief explanation of blockchain security features

2. Trip Planning

Step 1: Destination Exploration

  • Browse featured destinations in Jharkhand
  • Explore categories (wildlife, waterfalls, tribal villages, temples)
  • View VR previews of key attractions
  • Read about local culture and traditions

Step 2: Trip Parameters

  • Set travel dates and duration
  • Specify budget constraints
  • Indicate group size and composition
  • Select transportation preferences

Step 3: AI-Generated Itinerary

  • System generates personalized itinerary based on preferences
  • Day-by-day breakdown of activities and attractions
  • Estimated costs and time requirements
  • Alternative suggestions for each day

Step 4: Itinerary Customization

  • Add, remove, or reorder activities
  • Adjust time allocations
  • Save multiple itinerary versions
  • Share itinerary with travel companions

3. Booking & Payments

Step 1: Accommodation Booking

  • Browse verified homestays, hotels, and eco-lodges
  • View detailed information, photos, and blockchain-verified reviews
  • Check availability for selected dates
  • Make reservation with secure blockchain payment

Step 2: Activity & Experience Booking

  • Select guided tours, cultural experiences, and adventure activities
  • Book with verified local guides and service providers
  • Secure payment through blockchain escrow system
  • Receive digital tickets and confirmation

Step 3: Transportation Arrangements

  • Book local transportation options
  • Arrange for guides and drivers
  • View real-time availability and schedules
  • Receive route maps and contact information

Step 4: Payment Confirmation

  • Review all bookings and costs
  • Complete payment through secure blockchain transaction
  • Receive smart contract details for each booking
  • Download booking confirmations and vouchers

4. During the Trip

Step 1: Real-Time Navigation

  • Access offline maps for remote areas
  • Follow turn-by-turn directions to attractions
  • Receive real-time updates on transport schedules
  • Get alerts about road conditions or closures

Step 2: AR Exploration

  • Use AR mode to scan surroundings for information
  • View historical overlays on monuments and sites
  • Identify flora and fauna in natural areas
  • Access contextual stories and information

Step 3: Local Marketplace

  • Discover nearby artisans and handicraft sellers
  • Purchase authentic local products with blockchain verification
  • Learn about the cultural significance of purchases

Step 4: Real-Time Assistance

  • Chat with AI assistant for information and recommendations
  • Request emergency assistance if needed
  • Modify itinerary based on changing conditions
  • Connect with local guides and service providers

5. Post-Trip

Step 1: Service Confirmation

  • Confirm completion of booked services
  • Release payments to service providers
  • Report any issues with services
  • Access dispute resolution if necessary

Step 2: Feedback & Reviews

  • Rate and review accommodations, guides, and experiences
  • Share photos and stories from the trip
  • Provide suggestions for improving services
  • Contribute to the blockchain-verified review system

Step 3: Digital Souvenirs

  • Access digital collection of NFTs for purchased handicrafts
  • View trip memories and statistics
  • Share experiences on social media
  • Download personalized trip report

Step 4: Future Planning

  • Receive personalized recommendations for future trips
  • Save favorite locations and experiences
  • Earn loyalty rewards for next visits
  • Participate in community forums and discussions
