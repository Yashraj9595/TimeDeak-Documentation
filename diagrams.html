System Diagrams

This section contains comprehensive diagrams that illustrate the architecture, user flows, and system components of the Pravas tourism platform. These visual representations help understand the complex interactions between different modules and stakeholders.

Tourist Journey Diagram

This diagram illustrates the complete tourist journey through the Pravas platform, from initial discovery to post-trip engagement. It shows how tourists interact with different features and services throughout their travel experience, highlighting the seamless integration of AI-powered recommendations, AR/VR experiences, and blockchain-secured transactions.

Journey Stages:

  • Discovery & Planning: AI-powered destination recommendations, itinerary planning, and service discovery
  • Booking & Preparation: Secure blockchain transactions, AR/VR previews, and travel preparation
  • On-Site Experience: Real-time navigation, AR overlays, local marketplace interactions, and weather alerts
  • Post-Trip Engagement: Feedback submission, social sharing, and future trip planning

Use Case Diagram

This use case diagram shows the different actors and their interactions with the Pravas system. It identifies the main functionalities available to tourists, local vendors, administrators, and other stakeholders.

Main Actors:

  • Tourists: Primary users who plan trips, book services, and explore destinations
  • Local Vendors: Service providers who list their offerings on the marketplace
  • Administrators: Platform managers who oversee operations and content
  • AI System: Automated services for recommendations and support

User Flow Diagram

This user flow diagram illustrates the step-by-step journey of a tourist using the Pravas platform, from initial registration to completing their trip. It shows the decision points, interactions, and possible paths through the system.

Key Flow Stages:

  • Onboarding: Registration, profile setup, and preference configuration
  • Planning: Destination selection, itinerary creation, and service booking
  • Experience: Real-time navigation, AR/VR previews, and local interactions
  • Completion: Feedback submission, payment processing, and trip documentation
